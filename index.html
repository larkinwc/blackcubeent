Is He Gone?


Born 1977 in Harlem, New York, Levan grew up during the infamous 80’s and 90’s “Crack Era” where he came out with more life experience than one could ever imagine, thus he shares his experience with the world. In 2001, Levan enlisted into the U.S. Navy where he has continued to actively serve for the past 15 years. He is most notably recognized for his debut biography, “Is He Gone” which has received much praise and acknowledgment throughout the book community.

Levan has over 6 years of extensive experience in creating books and writing scripts. With his knowledge and expertise, Levan has created Black Cube Entertainment, a book publication and screenwriting company, to assist writers in producing their stories.
